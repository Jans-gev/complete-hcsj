    our humble origins

    Octaloop started out within the humble confines of a hostel room shared by two partners/ freelancers in 2008. Acquiring a number of foreign clients, within four years we laid the cornerstone of our first office. After hiring our first employee, we never looked back and continued flourishing/ diversifying our skillset. Our clients are from all over the world , from Malaysia to the UK and USA . With a promising future ahead, Octaloop continues to grow its roots.
